Mathayom 6 IELTS Quizzes for Grade 9 Students

Instructions: Creating IELTS Questions Using Adobe Captivate

Objective:

Students will use Adobe Captivate to design interactive IELTS questions for the Reading or Writing sections. The questions should simulate real IELTS exam tasks and provide a user-friendly interface for learners to practice.
